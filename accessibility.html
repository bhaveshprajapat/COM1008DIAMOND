Accessibility

During the development of the website we aimed to achieve World Web Consortium (W3C) AA standard, to ensure that our website was accessible to all. We believe we achieved this by providing non-text Content a text alternative, ensuring that all our content has a meaningful sequence, ensuring all colours non-decoration elements have a contrast ration of at least 4.5:1 and ensuring that our website could be successfully used by a screen reader. We believed that due to the results of WAVE and our own self-assessment against W3C’s AA guidelines that we have achieved a AA standard for accessibility
