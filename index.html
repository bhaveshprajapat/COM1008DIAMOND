Welcome to Computer Science at The Diamond

This is your guide to one of the most important buildings at the University of Sheffield.

This £81 million project, opening its doors in 2015, is the highlight for the University of Sheffield and has been quoted a unique and inspiring facility that won the design through innovation award in the 2016 Yorkshire and Humber Region Royal Institute of Chartered Surveyors award. During this guide we hope to show you what it is like working in the Diamond and the great number of facilites that the Diamond has to offer to you during your time at the University of Sheffield.
